The Band:

Pearl Jam is an American hard rock band that was most popular during the 90's, and was most well-known as the big 4 bands of the grunge movement along with Nirvana, Alice In Chains, Soundgarden. Their most popular songs outside of this album are Last Kiss, Yellow Ledbetter, and Daughter.

Most Popular Songs:

Alive
The most popular song on the ablum with over 330 million plays on Spotify. It is the third track on the ablum and was Pearl Jam's first released single and gave them the attention that led to their future popularity.
Even Flow
The second most popular song on the album with over 310 million plays on Spotify. It is the second track of the album and is about a homeless person and how he copes with his situation.
Black
The third most popular song with over 270 million plays on Spotify. It is a ballad type song about first relationships. The song was a non-single, making its popularity striking.

Why The Album Is Important

This album was one of the earliest and most popular albums of the grunge movement, released before Nirvana's "Nevermind" made the movement massively popular. The album was very influential toward alternative music and had the strongest classic rock influences out of the other big grunge groups. For me, this album is very nostalgic as it was played often around my house while growing up.
