The Band:

Thin Lizzy is an Irish hard rock band that was most popular during the 70's, with a couple hits in the early 80's. Their most popular songs outside of this album are Dancing In the Moonlight, Whiskey In The Jar, and Don't Believe A Word.

Most Popular Songs:

The Boys Are Back In Town
The most popular song on the ablum with over 200 million plays on Spotify. It is the sixth track on the ablum and was included as number 272 on Rolling Stone's "500 Greatest Songs Of All Time"
Jailbreak
The second most popular song on the album with over 40 million plays on Spotify. It is the first and title track of the album. It has appeared on numerous movies and was named 73rd best hard rock song of all time by VH1.
Cowboy Song
The third most popular song with over 15 million plays on Spotify. The song transitions from a softer, country influenced guitar riff to a full-on hard rock song over time.
And more...
Other great songs on this album are Angel From The Coast, Romeo and The Lonely Girl, and Emerald

Why The Album Is Important

This album is important because it popularized the use of twin-guitar harmonies and the song lyrics had great storytelling. This storytelling is clearly evident in songs like "Cowboy Song", detailing the story of a cowboy traveling through to the US. I've always been fond of "The Boys Are Back In Town" since I was a kid, and after hearing the rest of the album it certainly matches up.
