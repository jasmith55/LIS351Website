The Band:

Def Leppard is a British hard rock band that was most popular during the 80's and exploded in popularity with their 1987 album "Hysteria". Their most popular songs outside of this album are Pour Some Sugar On Me, Hysteria, Photograph, and Love Bites.

Most Popular Songs:

Bringin' On The Heartbreak
The most popular song on the ablum with over 15 million plays on Spotify. It is the fourth track on the album and was one of the first vidoes to be aired repeatedly on MTV, kickstarting Def Leppard's future popularity.
Let It Go
The second most popular song on the album with over 6 million plays on Spotify. It is the opening track and was the first single Def Leppard released from this album.
High 'N' Dry (Saturday Night)
The third most popular song with over 3 million plays on Spotify. It is the third and title track of the album. This song made the Parents Music Resource Center(PRMC)'s "Filthy Fifteen" for lyrics about alcohol use.
And more...
Other great songs on this album are Lady Strange, Switch 625, Mirror Mirror, and On Through The Night

Why The Album Is Important

This album is important because it combined heavy instrumentals with pop hooks and helped kickstart the glam metal genre. This genre was very popular in the 80's with bands like Bon Jovi, Motley Crue, and Guns N' Roses. Personally, I enjoy this album more than the later Def Leppard albums because of its more raw and heavy sound.
